I) What is Mongoose 
    1) A way to connect express and node to our MongoDB.
    2) Known as a ODM 
        A) Object Data Mapper 
            i) ODMs map documents coming from a db into usable JS objects
            ii) Mongoose provides ways for us to model out our application
            data and define a schema. It offers easy ways to validate data and 
            build complex queries from the comfort of JS
